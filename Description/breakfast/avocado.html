Avocado Toast with Hemp Seeds and Microgreens

A nourishing, protein-packed avocado toast topped with crunchy seeds and fresh microgreens.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 slices whole-grain or sourdough bread, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon hemp seeds
  • Microgreens, for garnish
  • Red pepper flakes, for a little heat (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread the avocado mixture onto the toasted bread.
  3. Sprinkle with hemp seeds, top with microgreens, and a pinch of red pepper flakes if desired.
  4. Serve immediately for a fresh, energizing breakfast.
