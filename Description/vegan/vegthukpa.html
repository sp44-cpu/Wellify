Vegan Thukpa

A comforting Himalayan noodle soup filled with vegetables and spices.

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili paste (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside
  2. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Saut√© the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add the carrots, bell pepper, and mushrooms. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth, soy sauce, and chili paste. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the spinach and cooked noodles. Stir until the spinach wilts.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime juice.
