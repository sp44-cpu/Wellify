Cauliflower Buffalo Wings

A spicy and crispy alternative to traditional buffalo wings, made with cauliflower.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1/2 cup flour (or gluten-free flour)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon melted vegan butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk: In a bowl, whisk together flour, water, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Dip: Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, coating thoroughly. Place on the baking sheet.
  4. Bake: Bake for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  5. Oven Time: In a separate bowl, mix hot sauce and melted vegan butter. Toss the baked cauliflower in the sauce and return to the oven for 10 more minutes.
  6. Enjoy: Serve warm with vegan ranch dressing or celery sticks
