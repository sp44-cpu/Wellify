Chickpea and Spinach curry

A protein-packed, creamy curry perfect for weeknight dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat coconut oil over medium heat. Saut√© the onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.
  2. Stir in the curry powder and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the chickpeas and coconut milk. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in the spinach until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot over rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.
